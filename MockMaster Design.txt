- Answer sections: Slightly darker background for distinction
- AI-generated content: Subtle glow or gradient border to indicate AI

**Spacing & Layout:**
- Use consistent spacing scale (4px, 8px, 12px, 16px, 24px, 32px, 48px)
- Generous padding for breathing room
- Max-width containers (1280px) for readability

- Subtle box shadows: shadow-sm on cards, shadow-md on elevated elements
